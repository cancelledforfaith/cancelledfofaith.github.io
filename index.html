Stand with Marc: Fight for Faith and Justice

Marc, a devout Catholic, was wrongfully terminated from his job at Haven Hospice for expressing his faith. He faces an uphill battle for justice.

Marc's Story

Marc worked tirelessly at Haven, sharing his faith and compassion with patients and their families. However, his faith was met with hostility, leading to unjust repercussions.

  • Workshop Incident

    Marc shared his faith during a volunteer workshop, leading to reprimands and threats to his job.

  • Medical Hardship

    Facing a serious health issue, Marc was forced to take medical leave. Haven retaliated by revoking his leave, canceling his insurance and firing him.

  • Legal Battles

    Marc is pursuing a case against Haven for religious discrimination, FMLA interference, and defamation. He is seeing justice against a powerful corporation.
